# MHacks 15 Best Medical Hack Award Winner

HealthMS is a tool to allow Multiple Sclerosis patients to log and monitor their symptoms and health data with a builtin suite for determining correlations between data. It allows users to demystify their healthcare data, analyze effectiveness of treatments, find potential confounds, and communicate with an AI-powered Healthcare Assistant chatbot to discuss potential correlations.
